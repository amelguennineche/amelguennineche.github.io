Discover Ras Mohammed

Ras Mohammed is where the desert meets the sea in its purest form. Located at the southern tip of the Sinai Peninsula, this protected national park is home to some of the most vibrant coral reefs in the world.

Crystal-clear waters, dramatic underwater landscapes, and an incredible diversity of marine life make every dive feel like entering a living ocean world.

It is not just a diving destination — it is a natural sanctuary where silence, color, and life exist in perfect harmony.

Why dive here

Experience one of the most iconic marine parks in the Red Sea with unmatched coral biodiversity.
