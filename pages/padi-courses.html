        Focused programs to sharpen specific skills beyond your core certifications.

        Deep Diver

        Specialty Course

        Go beyond recreational limits with the skills to plan and execute deeper dives safely. This specialty builds the confidence and procedures needed to explore sites between 18 and 40 metres. It is essential for divers who want to access deeper reefs and walls responsibly.

        Duration

        2 Days

        Course Includes

        • 4 Open Water Dives

        What You'll Learn

        • Planning deep dives
        • Managing gas consumption
        • Understanding narcosis
        • Using backup procedures
        Prerequisites

        Advanced Open Water Diver

        Book Deep Diver Specialty

        Enriched Air Nitrox

        Specialty Course

        Dive smarter with enriched air nitrox — the most popular PADI specialty worldwide. Learn to extend bottom time and shorten surface intervals while diving within safe oxygen limits. A valuable certification for any active diver.

        Duration

        1 Day

        Course Includes

        • Theory + Practical Session

        What You'll Learn

        • Oxygen analysis
        • Dive computer configuration
        • Nitrox dive planning
        Prerequisites

        Open Water Diver

        Book Enriched Air Nitrox Specialty

        Wreck Diver

        Specialty Course

        Discover the thrill of exploring sunken vessels with proper techniques and safety procedures. This specialty teaches responsible wreck diving from exterior surveys to limited penetration. Perfect for divers drawn to history and adventure underwater.

        Duration

        2–3 Days

        Course Includes

        • 4 Open Water Dives

        What You'll Learn

        • Safe wreck exploration
        • Mapping wrecks
        • Penetration techniques
        • Reel and guideline use
        Prerequisites

        Advanced Open Water Diver

        Book Wreck Diver Specialty

        Specialty Course

        What You'll Learn

        • Solving common gear problems

        • Equipment selection
