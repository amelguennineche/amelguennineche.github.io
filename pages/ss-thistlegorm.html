Dive Into History – SS Thistlegorm

The SS Thistlegorm is one of the most famous wreck dives in the world, resting in the depths of the Red Sea since World War II. Once a British cargo ship, it now lies preserved underwater, transformed into a time capsule of history.

Inside and around the wreck, divers can discover astonishing remains of military cargo such as trucks, motorcycles, and supplies, all frozen in time. Every dive feels like exploring a submerged museum where history and nature meet.

This is not just a dive — it is a journey back in time, offering one of the most iconic wreck diving experiences on the planet.

Why dive here

Explore a legendary WWII wreck filled with history, marine life, and one of the most unique diving experiences in the world.
