Discover Tiran Island

Tiran Island lies in the crystal-clear waters of the Gulf of Aqaba, where vibrant coral reefs and deep blue horizons create one of the most breathtaking marine landscapes in the Red Sea.

This island is surrounded by untouched dive sites, rich in marine life and colorful coral gardens. Every dive here feels calm, peaceful, and full of natural beauty, making it perfect for both relaxation and exploration.

It is a hidden paradise where the ocean reveals its purest form — quiet, vast, and alive.

Why dive here

Discover pristine coral reefs and calm waters in one of the most beautiful island environments of the Red Sea.
